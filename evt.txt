# Hawaii Food & Business Events Calendar 2025-2026

## MAJOR FESTIVALS & EVENTS

### Made in Hawaii Festival
**Date:** August 15-17, 2025 (Friday-Sunday)  
**Location:** Honolulu, Oahu  
**Venue:** Neal Blaisdell Center  
**Benefits:** Showcase Hawaii-made products, network with 40,000+ attendees  
**Perfect for:** Local food producers, specialty foods, value-added products  
**Vendor Application Deadline:** July 15, 2025  

### Hawaii Food & Wine Festival
**Date:** October 17 - November 2, 2025 (3 weekends)  
**Location:** Multiple islands (Oahu, Maui, Big Island)  
**Venue:** Multiple venues across islands  
**Benefits:** Premier culinary networking, educational seminars, media exposure  
**Perfect for:** Restaurants, chefs, beverage professionals, food suppliers  
**Participation:** Various levels from sponsorship to vendor booths

## COST ESTIMATES

### Farmers Markets
- Weekly booth fees: $25-75 per day
- Annual permits: $50-200
- Setup costs: $200-1,000 initial investment

### Major Events
- Made in Hawaii Festival: $500-2,500 booth fees
- Hawaii Food & Wine Festival: $1,000-10,000+ (varies by participation level)
- Pop-up events: $100-500
